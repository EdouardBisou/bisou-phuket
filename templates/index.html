06 FAQ

Good to know

Where is Bisou Phuket?

Bisou Phuket is at No.4 Bandon-Cherngtalay Road, Choeng Thale, Thalang, Phuket 83110, in the Cherngtalay area close to Bang Tao and Laguna. Free valet parking is available.

What kind of restaurant is Bisou Phuket?

A modern French restaurant. Chef and co-founder Antoine Darquin serves an author's cuisine with French roots and plates built to share, alongside a French cellar by sommelier Théo Lavergne. Simple & sexy.

What are the opening hours?

Dinner nightly, from 5:30 PM to midnight. Last food order is at 11 PM.

How do I book a table?

Reserve online with the booking button, or call {{phone}}. Weekends usually fill up about a week ahead.

What are the signature dishes?

Truffle French toast, house Beef Wellington, salt-crust seabass flambeed at the table, and caviar linguine. The menu evolves with the market while a few signatures stay on.

Is there parking?

Yes, free valet parking for all guests.

Does Bisou Phuket host private events?

Yes. A private dining room, a second floor, or a full venue buyout for weddings, corporate dinners, birthdays and celebrations, with custom menus and paired wines. See private events.

Is Bisou good for a date night?

Yes. The kiss-wall dining room, candlelight and a French cellar make Bisou one of the more romantic spots in Phuket for couples and special occasions.
