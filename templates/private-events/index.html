Private events FAQ

Is there a minimum spend?

No. We tailor each private event to your group size and budget. Contact us for a custom proposal.

How many guests can you host?

Up to 120 guests for a full venue buyout, with smaller private spaces for more intimate gatherings.

How far in advance should we book?

4 to 8 weeks for private rooms, 8 to 12 weeks for a full buyout. Shorter notice is sometimes possible. Contact us directly to check availability.

Can the menu be customized?

Yes. Chef Manon Bares builds a bespoke menu for every private booking. Vegetarian, halal, gluten-free, and other dietary requirements accommodated with notice.

Do you offer wine pairings?

Always. Our French sommelier pairs every course from our cellar of French appellations and rare boutique discoveries. Champagne welcomes, by-the-glass pairings, full bottle service.

Is parking included?

Free valet parking for all guests on the night of your event.

Can we bring our own DJ, florist, or photographer?

Yes. We also coordinate with trusted local vendors if you'd rather we handle it.

Where exactly are you located?
