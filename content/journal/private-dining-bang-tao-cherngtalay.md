For a private group dinner in Bang Tao or Cherngtalay, the most characterful room is Bisou, a modern French restaurant near Laguna that opens as a private dining room, a private upper floor or a full-venue buyout. Nearby, SUAY Cherngtalay hosts private dinners in its courtyard, Tatonka takes private parties near Boat Avenue, and PRU at Trisara keeps a ten-seat room inside its wine cellar. As of July 2026, these are the rooms to know for a milestone, a wedding or a corporate dinner on Phuket's west coast.

Cherngtalay and Bang Tao lean quiet after the beach, so the handful of rooms that hold a real private event are worth knowing by name. Below, each is placed by what it genuinely offers a group, with a clean route to booking yours.

## Three private spaces at Bisou

Bisou opens three private options at No.4 Bandon-Cherngtalay Road, from an intimate curtained room to the whole house, each shaped to your group rather than a fixed number. There is no set minimum and no hard ceiling: head chef Manon Bares builds the menu around your night, the French sommelier pairs it, sauces are flambeed at the table and the meat is carved in front of your guests.

## How to choose your private dinner

Choose by the occasion. For an intimate milestone or a honeymoon dinner, the curtained private room at Bisou keeps the evening close. For a mid-sized celebration with room to move, the private upper floor works, joined to the bar by the spiral staircase. For a wedding or an end-of-year party, a full-venue buyout gives you the whole house and a bar that runs to 1 AM. Whichever you pick, head chef Manon Bares builds the menu around your guests and your budget. [Tell us about your event](/private-events) and we shape the room to your group.

## Frequently asked questions

### Where can I host a private group dinner near Bang Tao?

For a private group dinner near Bang Tao and Laguna, Bisou in Cherngtalay opens a private dining room, a private upper floor or a full-venue buyout at No.4 Bandon-Cherngtalay Road. Nearby alternatives include SUAY Cherngtalay, Tatonka near Boat Avenue, and PRU at Trisara.

### How many guests can Bisou host for a private event?

Bisou shapes the space to your group rather than a fixed number, from an intimate private table to a full-venue buyout of the whole restaurant. Share your headcount and the occasion, and the team confirms the right space.

### Is there a minimum spend for a private event at Bisou Phuket?

Bisou tailors every private event to your group and your budget, with no fixed minimum. Contact the team through the private events page for a proposal built around your evening.

### Can the menu be customized for a private dinner?

Yes. Head chef Manon Bares designs a bespoke menu for every private booking, with wine pairings by a French sommelier, and vegetarian, halal and other dietary needs handled with notice.

### How far ahead should I book a private room in Phuket?

Plan on roughly four to eight weeks for a private room and longer for a full-venue buyout, especially in high season. Shorter notice is sometimes possible, so it is worth asking directly to check availability.
